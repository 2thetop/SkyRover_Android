SmartRover is the drone control program. This program is the easiest way to fly our small unmanned aircraft SkyRover, are connected to the SkyRover through the BLE 4.0 and has a range of remote controls approximately 25m.

    Always keep the Skyrover in sight. Do not children, pets, or fly near fragile objects. Please always maintain a minimum distance of about 2m (80 inch) between the propeller man, pets, or other objects to rotate. When operating the Multirotor child may require adult help. Do not heat or expose to flame.
